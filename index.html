Who we are

Rooted in aloha.
Reaching for tomorrow.

Pili Pū — "to come together as one" — was founded on a simple belief: that every person in our community deserves access to fresh food, a safe home, personalized healthcare, quality education, and a path to economic opportunity.

From keiki to kūpuna, our work is guided by the conviction that technology, placed in the hands of community, becomes a tool for collective rise.

What we do

Two pillars. One purpose.

ʻĀina
The land · the earth · all that sustains life

ʻĀina is the foundation of everything. It is the land beneath our feet, the water that flows through our valleys, the air that carries life across our islands. Our work honors this interdependence — nurturing the earth, the ocean, and the sky as living systems worthy of care, restoration, and protection for all who come after us.

Poʻe
The people · the community · stewards of the whole

Poʻe are the people — the community as a whole, bound together in shared responsibility. Our work invests in people as the primary stewards of ʻĀina: equipping them with knowledge, tools, and opportunity to care for their environment, their families, and one another across every generation.

Projects and programs are currently being developed across both pillars to bring this vision to life. We invite partners, funders, and community members to join us at the beginning of something meaningful.

"Pili Pū — to come together as one. Guided by Makaliʻi, grounded in aloha, building a future that belongs to all of us."
